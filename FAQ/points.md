# Points — Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I earn points?

A1: Earn points by making eligible purchases with partner merchants, participating in promotions, and completing in-app campaigns. Each campaign lists its earning rules.

Q2: When are points credited to my account?

A2: Points are usually credited after the transaction is confirmed. Some promotions credit points instantly; others may take up to several business days.

Q3: Can points be transferred to other users?

A3: Point transfers are not typically supported. If a transfer or gifting feature exists, it will be documented in the app and in the reward terms.

Q4: What happens to points after a refund?

A4: Points from refunded purchases will be deducted according to the refund policy. Updates to your points balance may take a few days.

Q5: How can I view my points activity?

A5: In the app go to Wallet → Points History to see earned, redeemed, and expired entries with transaction references and timestamps.
