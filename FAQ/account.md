# Account — Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I create a Joy Loyalty account?

A1: Open the Joy Loyalty app, tap "Sign up", enter your phone number or email, create a secure password, and follow the verification steps (SMS or email code). After verification you can complete your profile.

Q2: I didn't receive the verification code. What should I do?

A2: Check your network signal and that the phone number or email is correct. Wait 2–3 minutes, then request a new code. If you still don't receive it, try email verification or contact Joy Support with your device details.

Q3: How can I change my phone number or email?

A3: Go to Settings → Account → Contact Info. Enter the new phone or email and complete the verification step (SMS/email) to confirm the change.

Q4: I forgot my password — how can I reset it?

A4: On the login screen tap "Forgot password", provide your registered email or phone, then follow the reset link or code sent to you. Choose a new strong password once verified.

Q5: How do I permanently delete my account?

A5: In the app, navigate to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account and follow the instructions. Deleting an account may remove access to active rewards; contact Support if you have pending redemptions.
