# Privacy & Security — Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What data does Joy Loyalty collect?

A1: The app collects necessary data for service delivery: name, email, phone, transaction history, device metadata, and optional profile preferences.

Q2: How is my data used?

A2: Data is used to operate the loyalty program, personalize offers, process rewards, and for analytics and fraud prevention. Aggregated data may be used for insights.

Q3: Will my data be shared with partners?

A3: Joy may share data with partners under data processing agreements. Any marketing sharing requires user consent where applicable.

Q4: How do I request a data export or deletion?

A4: Go to Settings → Privacy → Data Requests to request export or deletion. Follow the guided steps; deletion may be subject to retention rules.

Q5: What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access?

A5: Immediately change your password, enable any available 2FA, and contact Support with details. Support can temporarily suspend the account while investigating.
