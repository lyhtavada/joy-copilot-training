# Rewards — Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What types of rewards are available?

A1: Joy Loyalty rewards typically include discount vouchers, cashback credits, partner offers, and exclusive promotional access. Available rewards vary by campaign and region.

Q2: How do I see which rewards I can claim?

A2: Open the Rewards tab in the app to view the catalog. Each reward shows the point cost, expiry date, and any usage conditions.

Q3: Can I combine multiple rewards on a single purchase?

A3: Combining rewards depends on merchant and campaign rules. The app will notify you at checkout if multiple rewards cannot be applied together.

Q4: What if a reward I claimed doesn't appear at checkout?

A4: Verify that the reward is active, not expired, and that you meet merchant restrictions (e.g., minimum spend). If it still doesn't apply, take a screenshot and contact Support.

Q5: Do claimed rewards expire?

A5: Yes — many rewards have expiration dates. Check the reward details for expiry and terms. Expired rewards cannot be redeemed.
