### [hashtag](#overview) Overview

Joy Loyalty offers four pricing tiers designed to scale with your business — from new stores testing loyalty programs to enterprise brands managing complex omnichannel experiences. All plans include a 14-day free trial (except Starter), the Ultimate plan goes with 30-day free-trial and pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

---

### [hashtag](#pricing-structure) Pricing structure

Joy Loyalty uses a **base subscription + order volume** pricing model:

- **Base monthly fee** — Covers access to all plan features
- **Free monthly orders** — Each plan includes a quota of orders at no extra cost
- **Overage fees** — Additional orders beyond your quota are charged per 100 orders

### [hashtag](#frequently-asked-questions) Frequently asked questions

#### [hashtag](#general-pricing-questions) General pricing questions

**Do I need to pay anything to get started?**
No. Joy offers a free Starter plan forever with 150 monthly orders and basic features. You can upgrade to paid plans anytime.

**Do you offer a free trial for paid plans?**
Yes. All paid plans (Essential, Advanced, Ultimate) include a 14-day (30-day with the Ultimate plan) free trial with full feature access and no credit card required.

#### [hashtag](#usage-control-questions) Usage control questions

**How can I control my usage?**

- Monitor your order volume in Joy dashboard
- Set up quota alerts (email notifications)
- Exclude non-loyalty orders by adjusting earning rules
- Upgrade to a higher plan for better overage rates
- Time high-volume campaigns strategically

**Can I set a hard limit on orders to avoid overage fees?**
No hard limits, but you can set up alerts when approaching 80% and 100% of your quota.

#### [hashtag](#plan-change-questions) Plan change questions

**Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

**What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
Your data remains intact. Features from the higher plan become unavailable, but you can upgrade again anytime to regain access.

**Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
Currently, Joy only offers monthly billing. Contact sales for annual contract options.

#### [hashtag](#custom-and-enterprise-questions) Custom and enterprise questions

**Are there custom plans available for high-volume stores?**
Yes. Stores with 5,000+ monthly orders or unique needs can contact sales for custom enterprise pricing with volume discounts.

**What is "Priority support"?**
Priority support includes faster response times, live chat access, and dedicated support channels.

**Do you offer agency or partner pricing?**
Yes. Joy has a partner program with special pricing for agencies managing multiple client stores. [Learn more about the Partner Program →arrow-up-right](https://joy.so/partners/)
