### [hashtag](#why-do-you-need-product-referral-block) Why do you need product referral block?

The **Product Referral** shows customers what they’ll get for referring friends, making them more likely to share and buy — helping merchants drive more sales, gain new customers, and grow without extra ad costs

### [hashtag](#how-to-set-up-the-product-referral) How to set up the Product Referral

1

**Access Embedded Content**

- Open the **Joy Loyalty** app.
- Navigate to **Embedded Content** from the dashboard.

2

**Click Product page, select Product referral**

- Choose **Product referral** and click **Edit in Theme Editor**

3

**Add the Block to Your Loyalty Page**

- Open the **Theme Editor**
- Choose **Product page**
- Select **Add Block**, then find **Product** **Referral** under the **Apps** section
