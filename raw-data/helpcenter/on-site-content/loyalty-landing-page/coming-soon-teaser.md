### [hashtag](#what-is-coming-soon-teaser) What is "coming soon teaser"?

The "coming soon teaser" is your secret marketing weapon to create buzz and anticipation before launching your loyalty program. It's like a digital billboard that teases and excites potential customers about what's just around the corner.

### [hashtag](#why-do-you-need-this-feature) Why do you need this feature?

The "coming soon teaser" feature is essential for building excitement and anticipation around your upcoming loyalty program. It allows you to engage potential customers early, collect leads, and create buzz before the official launch.

### [hashtag](#how-to-set-up-coming-soon-teaser-to-your-loyalty-page) How to set up "coming soon teaser" to your loyalty page

1

**Open** **Embedded Content**

- Navigate to the **Embedded Content** section from the main menu.

2

**Edit in Theme Editor**

Click "**Edit in Theme Editor**" in the "coming soon teaser"

3

**Add the "coming soon teaser"**

- Select **Add Block** and choose **Joy: Coming soon teaser** from the apps category.
- Adjust the block’s position using drag-and-drop functionality.

4

**Customize the Content and Design**

- Modify the heading, table content, and colors to suit your brand.

5

**Save and Enable the Block**

- Save the changes to enable the block on your loyalty page.

### [hashtag](#ways-to-utilize-coming-soon-block) Ways to utilize "Coming soon" block

**Raise customer awareness**

- The "Coming Soon" block serves as a prominent notification, letting customers know that a loyalty program is on the horizon.
- Ensure the message is engaging and clear. Highlight key benefits of the upcoming program to captivate customer interest from the start.

**Collect leads**

- Merchants can integrate sign-up forms to collect customer emails or interest levels, enabling them to build a list of potential participants excited about the program.
- Offer an incentive for signing up, such as a first look at the rewards or exclusive early access. This increases the likelihood of customer participation.

**Boost customer expectations**

- Merchants can utilize the block to clearly communicate what customers can expect from the loyalty program, including details about rewards, benefits, and how to participate.
- Regularly update the block with new information or teasers as the launch date approaches to maintain interest and engagement.
