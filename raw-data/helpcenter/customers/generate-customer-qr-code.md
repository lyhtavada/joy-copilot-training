### [hashtag](#what-is-customer-qr-code) What is Customer QR Code?

A **Customer QR Code** is a unique barcode that contains essential information linked to a specific customer, such as their loyalty points, purchase history, and personal details. This code can be easily scanned using a POS system, allowing you to quickly access your custom ers' loyalty information and serve them better.

### [hashtag](#benefits-of-generating-customer-or-code) Why do you need this feature?

QR Codes revolutionize loyalty programs by offering instant, convenient access to customer information. They simplify transactions, personalize customer experiences, and provide businesses with quick insights while giving customers a modern, eco-friendly alternative to traditional loyalty cards.

### [hashtag](#how-to-generate-customer-qr-code) How to Generate Customer QR Code?

1

Open **Joy Loyalty** and choose **Customers** from navigation bar

2

Click **Generate pass wallet** to get a personalized loyalty pass for your customer

3

Go to **Google wallet** and toggle feature on

4

Click on **Google wallet** and set up depend on your preferences

5

Press **Save** to apply your changes

### [hashtag](#how-to-add-loyalty-pass-to-your-website) How to add Loyalty Pass to your website?

1

Go to **Themes**, select **Customize** to design your website content

2

Add block **Joy: Loyalty Pass Wallet** from Apps section to insert Google wallet

3

Click **Save**

### [hashtag](#how-your-clients-get-their-loyalty-pass) How Your Clients Get Their Loyalty Pass?

1

After signing up in the website, click on **Add to Google Wallet** to get quick access to Loyalty Pass

2

Press **Add**

3

Select **View pass** to have a closer look on your pass details
