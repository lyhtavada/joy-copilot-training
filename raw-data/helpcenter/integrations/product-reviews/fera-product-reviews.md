**Who can connect Joy with Fera Product Reviews?**

This feature is available for **All plans**.

### [hashtag](#how-to-integrate-with-fera) How to integrate with Fera?

The integration with Fera allows Joy's users to reward customers when they write a review for your store.

In this article, we will guide you on how to connect Joy with [Fera Product Reviews apparrow-up-right](https://apps.shopify.com/fera)

1

**Access Integration page**

From Joy app, please access integration menu > click to choose Fera Product Review

2

**Get Fera's secret key**

- Access Fera app > go to Settings page > App & Integrations > select API Keys

- At API Keys, copy Secret Key

3

**Enter Fera's Secret Key**

If you want to disconnect the 2 apps, please click on Disconnect button
