### [hashtag](#why-do-you-need-to-integrate-chatty-with-joy-loyalty) Why do you need to integrate Chatty with Joy Loyalty?

By integrating Chatty with Joy, you can:

1. **Get access to customer’s loyalty profile and activity right in Chatty Inbox**, which include:

- Type
- VIP tier
- Points balance
- Birthday
- Referral link

Then, you have more information to personalize answers to customer inquiries or provide suitable services/products.

You can check Joy profile in:

- Customer details in Contacts
- Conversation details

1. **Export FAQs about your loyalty program**

This will enhance Chatty's AI chatbot responses.

### [hashtag](#how-to-integrate-chatty-with-joy-loyalty) How to integrate Chatty with Joy Loyalty?

1

In the Joy Loyalty app, please access the **Integrations** section from the left menu and select **Chatty**. Then click on "**Go to app**".

2

**Get access** to customer’s loyalty profile and activity in Chatty Inbox

Click **Connect** to integrate Chatty with Joy Loyalty.

This will sync below information from Joy to Chatty:

- Type
- Birthday
- VIP tier
- Point balance
- Referral link

3

**Export FAQs** about your loyalty program

Click "Export FAQs as CSV" to export your loyalty program FAQs to enhance Chatty's AI chatbot responses
