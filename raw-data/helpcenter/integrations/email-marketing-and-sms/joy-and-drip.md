### [hashtag](#how-to-integrate-drip-with-the-joy-loyalty-app) **How to integrate Drip with the Joy Loyalty App**

1

Open the **Joy Loyalty dashboard** and navigate to the **Integrations** section.

- Select **Drip** from the list and click **Connect**.

2

**Authorize Drip access**

- Sign in to your Drip account and authorize access by clicking **Authorize.**

3

**Sync Loyalty Data**

- Once connected, check the integration status. If it shows **Connected**, data will sync automatically.
- If not, click the **Sync** button to manually sync loyalty data from **Joy Loyalty** to **Drip**.
