### [hashtag](#what-is-this-feature) What is this feature?

The [Joy Loyaltyarrow-up-right](https://apps.shopify.com/joyio?utm_source=orderlimit&utm_medium=banner_&utm_campaign=customer+limit_installs) and [Avada Order Limitarrow-up-right](https://apps.shopify.com/avada-order-limit) integration creates tiered product access based on customer loyalty status. When connected, these apps manage exclusive shopping experiences through automated customer tagging. As customers move up loyalty tiers, they gain access to exclusive products and collections you've designated for their tier level, creating a powerful incentive system that rewards your most loyal customers.

### [hashtag](#why-use-this-feature) Why use this feature

This integration transforms your loyalty program by adding exclusivity as a tangible benefit. Your customers gain compelling reasons to reach and maintain higher loyalty tiers to access exclusive products. This drives increased engagement with your loyalty program while boosting purchase frequency and average order values. The tiered access system also naturally segments your customers based on their purchasing behavior, allowing for more targeted marketing.

### [hashtag](#requirements-before-using-this-feature) Requirements before using this feature

2. The [Avada Order Limitarrow-up-right](https://apps.shopify.com/avada-order-limit) app is installed on your Shopify store
3. Products or collections you want to make exclusive to certain tiers

### [hashtag](#how-to-set-up-an-order-limit-for-vip-tiers) How to set up an order limit for VIP Tiers

Setting up product exclusivity for your VIP tiers involves three main steps: enabling automatic customer tagging, configuring tier privileges in Joy Loyalty, and setting up product restrictions in Order Limit.

1

**Enable VIP Tier Sync**

Navigate to **Membership** > **VIP Tier** in Joy Loyalty

In the **Tier configuration settings** section, check the box for **"Automatically tag VIP customers based on their tier."**

circle-info

This step is crucial for Order Limit to recognize the customer tier status

2

**Add Tier Privileges Exclusive products/collections**

Scroll down to the **Tier settings** section

For the tier you want to configure (e.g., Bronze), click the **"Add privileges"** button

From the privileges options, select **Exclusive products/collections**

Click **"Install"** in the banner to install or connect with the Avada Order Limit app

3

**Configure Order Limit**

Click **open Order Limit App**

Navigate to the **Limits management > Create new limit > Customer limit**

**Name Your Limit**: Enter a descriptive name for your limit (e.g., "Gold Tier Exclusive Products")

**Select Customer Tags to Limit**: Use the search field to find and select the VIP tier tags you want to exclude (e.g., find and exclude "bronze-tier" and "silver-tier" tags if creating Gold tier exclusives)

**Select Products to Limit**

- Choose whether to apply limits to:

  - All products (not recommended for tier exclusivity)
  - Specific products (recommended for creating tier-exclusive items)

  If choosing specific products, select the products or collections you want to make exclusive to this tier

4

**Finalize Privilege Setup**

Return to Joy Loyalty

Enter a description for the privilege that will be visible to customers

Save your changes

### [hashtag](#frequently-asked-questions) Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I make an entire collection exclusive to a specific tier?** A: Yes, you can select entire collections in Order Limit to restrict access based on loyalty tier tags.

**Q: What happens if a customer's tier status changes?** A: When a customer's tier changes, Joy Loyalty automatically updates their tags, which immediately affects their product access through Order Limit.

**Q: Can I set up temporary exclusivity for special events?** A: Yes, you can set start and end dates in Order Limit to create time-limited exclusive access.

**Q: How do customers know which products they can access?** A: It's best to create a dedicated page or collection for tier-exclusive products and clearly communicate the tier requirements in your loyalty program materials.

**Q: Can I combine tier-exclusive access with other loyalty rewards?** A: Yes, you can offer exclusive product access alongside other tier benefits like increased point earning rates or special discounts.
