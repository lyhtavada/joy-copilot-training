### [hashtag](#what-is-fill-out-a-survey-feature) What is "Fill out a survey" feature?

The Fill Out a Survey feature is a strategic feedback collection tool that connects your customer feedback process with your loyalty program. This feature automates the reward process for customers who complete your surveys, creating an automated feedback loop that benefits both your business and your customers.

### [hashtag](#why-do-you-need-this-feature) Why do you need this feature?

Feedback is a cornerstone for growth, offering insights into customer satisfaction, preferences, and areas for improvement. By incorporating Joy Loyalty's **Fill Out a Survey Rewards**, merchants can turn every completed survey into a win-win situation—valuable data for businesses and enticing rewards for customers.
