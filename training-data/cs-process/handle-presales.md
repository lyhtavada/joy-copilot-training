Q: A potential customer is asking about Joy before installing. What should I do?
Q: How do I handle pre-sales questions?
Q: A merchant asks "Does your app do X?" — what's the process?
Q: Someone asks to compare Joy with a competitor. How do I respond?
A: Pre-sales questions are high priority — this is a potential customer. SLA: respond within 2-4 hours.

**Step 1: Respond quickly**
Fast response shows good service and increases conversion.

**Step 2: Understand their needs**
- What are they trying to achieve with a loyalty program?
- What's their business model?
- What features are most important to them?

**Step 3: Provide clear information**
- Be honest about capabilities and limitations.
- Don't oversell — it leads to complaints later.
- Explain the value Joy brings to their specific use case.

**Step 4: Address concerns**
- Competitor comparison: focus on Joy's strengths without badmouthing others.
- Pricing objections: explain plan tiers and what's included.
- Technical limitations: be upfront and suggest workarounds.

**Step 5: Guide next steps**
- Encourage them to install the free plan and try it.
- Offer onboarding help and setup assistance.
- Share relevant help articles and resources.

**Step 6: Follow up**
- Check if they installed the app.
- Offer setup assistance.
- Track conversion.
