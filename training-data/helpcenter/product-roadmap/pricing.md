Q: What is Pricing in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How does Pricing work?
A: ### Who this guide is for

Shopify merchants evaluating Joy Loyalty or looking to understand their current plan and upgrade options.

---

### Overview

Joy Loyalty offers four pricing tiers designed to scale with your business — from new stores testing loyalty programs to enterprise brands managing complex omnichannel experiences. All plans include a 14-day free trial (except Starter), the Ultimate plan goes with 30-day free-trial and pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

---

### Pricing structure

Joy Loyalty uses a **base subscription + order volume** pricing model:

- **Base monthly fee** — Covers access to all plan features
- **Free monthly orders** — Each plan includes a quota of orders at no extra cost
- **Overage fees** — Additional orders beyond your quota are charged per 100 orders

---

### The four plans

500 monthly free orders, then +$15 per 100 additional orders

- Points for social engagement
- Dedicated loyalty page
- Analytics & reports
- Point expiration
- Shopify Flow and 30+ integrations

2,000 monthly free orders, then +$10 per 100 additional order
