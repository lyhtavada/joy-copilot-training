For Avada Order Limits merchants, [**install Joy Loyalty**](https://apps.shopify.com/joyio?utm_source=orderlimit&utm_medium=banner_&utm_campaign=customer+limit_installs) to design VIP tiers to segment customers better.

### What is this feature?

The [Joy Loyalty](https://apps.shopify.com/joyio?utm_source=orderlimit&utm_medium=banner_&utm_campaign=customer+limit_installs) and [Avada Order Limit](https://apps.shopify.com/avada-order-limit) integration creates tiered product access based on customer loyalty status. When connected, these apps manage exclusive shopping experiences through automated customer tagging. As customers move up loyalty tiers, they gain access to exclusive products and collections you've designated for their tier level, creating a powerful incentive system that rewards your most loyal customers.

### Why use this feature

This integration transforms your loyalty program by adding exclusivity as a tangible benefit. Your customers gain compelling reasons to reach and maintain higher loyalty tiers to access exclusive products. This drives increased engagement with your loyalty program while boosting purchase frequency and average order values. The tiered access system also naturally segments your customers based on their purchasing behavior, allowing for more targeted marketing.

### Requirements before using this feature

Before setting up this integration, you need:

1. An active Joy Loyalty account with [VIP Tiers](/membership/vip-tiers) configured
2. The [Avada Order Limit](https://apps.shopify.com/avada-order-limit) app is installed on your Shopify store
3. Products or collections you want to make exclusive to certain tiers

### How to set up an order limit for VIP Tiers

Setting up product exclusivity for your VIP tiers involves three main steps: enabling automatic customer tagging, configuring tier privileges in Joy Loyalty, and setting up product restrictions in Order Limit.

1

**Enable VIP Tier Sync**

Navigate to **Membership** > **VIP Tier** in Joy Loyalty

In the **Tier configuration settings** section, check the box for **"Automatically tag VIP customers based on their tier."**

This step is crucial for Order Limit to recognize the customer tier status

2

**Add Tier Privileges Exclusive products/collections**

Scroll down to the **Tier settings** section

For the tier you want to configure (e.g., Bronze), click the **"Add privileges"** button

From the privileges options, select **Exclusive products/collections**

Click **"Install"** in the banner to install or connect with the Avada Order Limit app

3

**Configure Order Limit**

Click **open Order Limit App**
