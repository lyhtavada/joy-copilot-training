### Why do you need this workflow?

By integrating Joy Loyalty with Klaviyo, you can send personalized emails that your customers will eagerly anticipate. This workflow guides you in creating and sending campaigns via your Klaviyo account using a specific list or segment and email template. Once you've successfully established the workflow between Joy Loyalty and Klaviyo, you'll be able to send your customers highly engaging communications.

**Dive into Shopify Flow and Create a New Workflow**
