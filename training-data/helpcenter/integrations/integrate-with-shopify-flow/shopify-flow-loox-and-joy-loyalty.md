Q: What is Shopify Flow Loox And Joy Loyalty in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Shopify Flow Loox And Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I connect Shopify Flow Loox And Joy Loyalty with Joy Loyalty?
A: This feature is available for **Professional, Advanced, and Enterprise plans**

### Why do you need this workflow?

The workflow between Joy Loyalty and Loox is essential for boosting your brand’s visibility by automatically showcasing customer reviews and star ratings on social media and search engines. This integration not only helps you build trust with potential customers but also incentivizes existing ones by awarding loyalty points for leaving reviews. By encouraging customer feedback and rewarding them, you strengthen engagement, enhance brand credibility, and foster long-term loyalty.

### How to create a workflow between Loox and Joy Loyalty?

1

**Access Shopify Flow and Create a New Workflow**

- You need to access Shopify Flow and create a new workflow. Here, you should choose Loox as the trigger

Step 1

2

**Select Action**

- Select '**Action**'. Here, you should choose 'Joy loyalty' to automatically add points for the new review

Step 2

And there you have it! You're all set to create a seamless workflow between Joy Loyalty and Loox.

Integrating Joy Loyalty with Loox is a powerful way to amplify your brand’s presence by displaying customer reviews on social media and search engines while rewarding customers with loyalty points.
