### Why do you need this workflow?

The workflow between Joy Loyalty and Stamped is essential for enhancing your store's credibility by automatically displaying customer reviews and star ratings, which can attract new customers and build trust with your audience. Furthermore, by rewarding customers with loyalty points each time they leave a review, you incentivize more engagement, encourage valuable feedback, and foster a stronger sense of loyalty. This integration not only streamlines the process but also strengthens your customer relationships, boosting satisfaction and contributing to the long-term success and growth of your business.

### How to create a workflow between Stamped and Joy Loyalty?

1

**Access Shopify Flow and Create a New Workflow**

- You need to access Shopify Flow and create a new workflow. Here, you should choose Reviews.io as the trigger

Choose Stamped as Trigger

2

**Select Action**

- Select '**Action**'. Here, you should choose 'Joy loyalty' to automatically add points for the new review

Select Action

And there you have it! You're all set to create a seamless workflow between Joy Loyalty and Revies.io
