Q: What is Shopify Flow Tydal And Joy Loyalty in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How does Shopify Flow Tydal And Joy Loyalty work?
Q: How do I connect Shopify Flow Tydal And Joy Loyalty with Joy Loyalty?
A: This feature is available for **Professional, Advanced, and Enterprise plans**

### How You Can Benefit from This Workflow[​](https://help.avada.app/Joy/AGettingStartted/how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-okendo/#how-you-can-benefit-from-this-workflow)

We're thrilled to introduce a new workflow between Joy Loyalty and Tydal. This workflow allows you to effortlessly display reviews and star ratings on social media and search engines. Isn't that fantastic?

What's more, once your customer successfully leaves their reviews on Tydal, the corresponding points will be added to their account in the Joy Loyalty Program. It's a win-win!

Tydal and Joy Loyalty Workflow

### Let's Create a Workflow between Joy Loyalty and Tydal[​](https://help.avada.app/Joy/AGettingStartted/how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-okendo/#lets-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-okendo)

#### Step 1: Access Shopify Flow and Create a New Workflow[​](https://help.avada.app/Joy/AGettingStartted/how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-okendo/#step-1-access-shopify-flow-and-create-a-new-workflow)

First things first, please access Shopify Flow and create a new workflow. Here, you'll need to choose Tydal as the trigger.

Shopify Flow

#### Step 2: Select Action - Choose Joy Loyalty[​](https://help.avada.app/Joy/AGettingStartted/how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-okendo/#step-2-select-action---choose-joy-loyalty)

Next, please select 'Action' and choose 'Joy loyalty' to automatically add points for each new review. And voila, you're all set!

Select Action

### How to choose Tydal as the review app in Write review program

1

**Get access to "Write review"**

Choose **Reward programs** -> **Earning programs** -> Click **Add rule** -> Choose **Write review**

2

**Choose review app**

**Select Tydal** as the review app to integrate with
