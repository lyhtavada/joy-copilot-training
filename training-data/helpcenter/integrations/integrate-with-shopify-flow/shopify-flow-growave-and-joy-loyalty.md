Q: What is Shopify Flow Growave And Joy Loyalty in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Shopify Flow Growave And Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I connect Shopify Flow Growave And Joy Loyalty with Joy Loyalty?
A: This feature is available for **Professional, Advanced, and Enterprise plans**

### Why do you need this workflow

The workflow between Joy Loyalty and Growave to seamlessly showcase customer reviews and star ratings on social media and search engines. This integration not only boosts your online presence but also rewards customers for leaving reviews by adding points to their Joy Loyalty Program account. It’s an effective way to enhance customer engagement while driving brand visibility and loyalty.

### How to create a workflow between Joy Loyalty and Growave?[​](https://help.avada.app/Joy/AGettingStartted/how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-growave/#how-to-create-a-workflow-between-joy-loyalty-and-growave)

1

**Access Shopify Flow and Create a New Workflow**

- You need to access Shopify Flow. Once there, create a new workflow and choose Growave as the trigger

Access Shopify Flow

2

**Select Action**

- Select the 'Action' option. Here, you should choose 'Joy loyalty' to automatically add points for a new review.

Select Action

### How to choose Growave as the review app in Write review program

1

**Get access to "Write review"**

Choose **Reward programs** -> **Earning programs** -> Click **Add rule** -> Choose **Write review**

2

**Choose review app**

**Select Growave** as the review app to integrate with

Want to set up a 'Write Review' program for your customers? Click [here](https://help.joy.so/reward-programs/earning-programs/write-review) for details

And voila! You've successfully created a workflow between Joy Loyalty and Growave.

### Wrap up

Integrating Joy Loyalty with Growave boosts your brand's visibility by showcasing reviews while rewarding customers with loyalty points, creating a win-win for both your business and customers.
