Q: What is Shopify Subscription in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Shopify Subscription?
Q: How do I connect Shopify Subscription with Joy Loyalty?
A: Who can use this feature?

- This feature is available in all plans

### Why do you need this feature?

Integrating **Joy Loyalty** with **Shopify Subscription** allows merchants to reward customers with loyalty points for subscribing and renewing their subscriptions. This powerful integration offers an effective incentive that not only encourages new sign-ups but also boosts customer retention by rewarding long-term commitment. By providing customers with tangible rewards for their ongoing subscriptions, businesses can foster deeper loyalty, enhance the customer experience, and strengthen lasting relationships.

### How to integrate Shopify Subscription with Joy Loyalty

1

Download Shopify Subscription apps from Shopify

2

Open Joy: Loyalty Program, go to **"Integrations"**, and select "**Shopify Subscription"**

3

Turn on connection next to Shopify Subscription. If successful, a Connected tag will appear

4

Click on “**Set Up**” to establish your reward program

5

Set up a reward program

- **Program name:** Customize the name of the reward program
- **Eligible customers:** Define which customers are eligible for the program
- **Rewarding method:** Choose between rewarding based on the amount spent or per purchase
- **Rewards value:** Set the reward points value for subscriptions.

6

Click on "**Turn on**" to save your reward program

Another way to set up a reward program for subscriptions (You still need to install and turn on Shopify subscription first). Go to "**Reward programs**" in the left menu. Use the Rule Engine to configure the Place Order rule by clicking "**Add more**." Then, select "**Subscription order**". Configure the reward points rule for customers in the Step 5 above.
