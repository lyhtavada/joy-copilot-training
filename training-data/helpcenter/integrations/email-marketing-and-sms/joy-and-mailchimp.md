A: **Who can connect Joy with Mailchimp?**

- This feature is available for **All plans**.

### Why do you need to integrate Mailchimp with Joy Loyalty Program?

Effective customer engagement requires a strategic approach, especially when it comes to loyalty programs. Integrating Joy Loyalty with Mailchimp empowers merchants to bridge the gap between loyalty data and email marketing. This integration allows for seamless sharing of customer data, enabling highly targeted and automated campaigns that cater to individual customer journeys.

### **Understanding the Rule**

Mailchimp is a powerful marketing platform that helps businesses connect with their audience through tailored email and SMS campaigns. Its core capabilities include:

- **Drag-and-Drop Campaign Creation:** Easily design professional emails with Mailchimp’s intuitive builder, with no coding required.
- **Audience Management:** Organize and segment customers based on various criteria, including loyalty status.
- **Automation:** Set up personalized email flows triggered by customer behavior or milestones.
- **Analytics and Reporting:** Monitor campaign performance to optimize future marketing efforts.
- **A/B Testing:** Experiment with different email versions to determine what resonates best with your audience.

By integrating Joy Loyalty with Mailchimp, merchants can seamlessly sync loyalty-related data to enhance their marketing efforts. This integration empowers businesses to create campaigns that speak directly to customers’ loyalty activities, fostering deeper engagement and higher conversions.

### **What Data Does Joy Loyalty Sync to Mailchimp?**

The following customer data is synced from Joy Loyalty to Mailchimp, enabling you to create more personalized and effective campaigns:

- **Joy Point Balance:** Show customers their current points to encourage redemptions.
- **Joy Member Status:** Identify whether a customer is enrolled in the loyalty program.
- **Joy Referral Link:** Include referral links in emails to boost word-of-mouth marketing.
- **Joy VIP Tier:** Highlight VIP status and offer exclusive rewards to premium members.
- **Joy Birthday:** Send personalized birthday greetings and special offers.

### **Benefits of Integrating Joy Loyalty and Mailchimp**

**1. Personalized Marketing Campaigns**

Loyalty data allows for highly targeted messaging, increasing the relevance and effectiveness of your campaigns. For example, you can send reminders about points balances or VIP tier benefits.

**2. Enhanced Segmentation**

Use customer data to segment audiences in Mailchimp. This enables more refined targeting based on loyalty points, referral status, or VIP tier, ensuring your messages reach the right people.
