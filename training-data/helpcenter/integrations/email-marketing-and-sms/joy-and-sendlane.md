Q: What is Joy And Sendlane in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Joy And Sendlane?
Q: How do I connect Joy And Sendlane with Joy Loyalty?
A: Personalized marketing is a powerful way to connect with your customers and boost sales. By integrating Joy Loyalty with Sendlane, you can sync customer data, including **Point Balance**, **Member Status**, **Referral link**, **Vip Tier** and **Birthday**, to create targeted, automated campaigns. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to set up the integration, so you can use your loyalty program for better segmentation and more effective email marketing. Let's dive in and get started!

### How to connect Joy with Sendlane

1

From the Joy Dashboard, click on "**Integrations**"

2

Find **Sendlane**, and click on it

3

Paste your **API Token**, then click "Connect"

4

**Select the list** to sync with the Joy Loyalty, then click "Connect"

### Where to find your API Token?

1

From your Sendlane Dashboard, click on **Account**, then choose **API**

2

Under **Sendlane API V2**, click the button **+ API Token**

3

Input a name for your token in the pop-up and click **Create**

4

Make sure to click **Copy API Token** and paste it into Joy

### What can you do by integrating Sendlane with Joy?

By syncing Joy's loyalty data to Sendlane, including:

- Point balance
- Member status
- Referral link
- VIP tier
- Birthday

You can:

1

**Segment customers** based on properties

2

Send **email marketing** with **personalized tags**

3

Send **automated transactional emails**

And that's it! Your integration between Joy Loyalty and Sendlane is now all set and ready to save your marketing efforts. If you run into any issues or have questions along the way, don't hesitate to reach out to our 24/7 live chat support.
