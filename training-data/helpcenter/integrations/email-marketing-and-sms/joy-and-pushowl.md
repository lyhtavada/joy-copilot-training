A: **Who can connect Joy with PushOwl?**

- This feature is available for **All** plans.

### Why do you need to integrate PushOwl with Joy Loyalty Program?

PushOwl is a leading Email and SMS marketing platform known for its intuitive segmentation tools and advanced push notification features. By integrating Joy Loyalty with PushOwl, merchants can automate push notifications for loyalty program events, such as birthdays, point expiration alerts, VIP tier updates, and more. This ensures customers are always engaged and informed, driving better retention and loyalty.

### **Understanding the Rule**

With this integration, merchants can automate push notifications for key loyalty milestones and activities. The integration supports various triggers, including:

- **7 Days Before Birthday**: Notify customers about their upcoming birthday and loyalty rewards.
- **Birthday Celebration**: Send warm birthday wishes along with loyalty points rewards.
- **Point Expiration Alerts**: Remind customers about expiring points (7 days and 3 days prior).
- **Eligible for Reward**: Alert customers when they have enough points to redeem a reward.
- **Point Activity Notifications**: Inform customers about earned or redeemed points.
- **VIP Tier Updates**: Keep customers updated about tier achievements, resets, or downgrades.

Each notification is fully customizable and designed to keep customers engaged with your brand, ensuring a seamless loyalty experience.

### How to integrate PushOwl with Joy Loyalty Program

1

Install both **Joy Loyalty** and **PushOwl** apps from the Shopify App Store.

2

Once both apps are installed, **the integration is automatic.**

There’s no need for additional configuration—Joy Loyalty will send loyalty program data to PushOwl, enabling notifications. If the integration doesn’t occur automatically, don’t hesitate to [contact our Customer Support team.](https://go.crisp.chat/chat/embed/?website_id=72a663b0-4cda-4e3b-8878-426bdd79364c)

3

Once connected successfully, **select loyalty program event triggers** to send notifications to your customers through PushOwl.
