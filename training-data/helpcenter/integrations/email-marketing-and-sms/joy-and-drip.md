### Why do you need to integrate Klaviyo with Joy?

Drip is a powerful email marketing automation platform tailored for e-commerce businesses, designed to enhance customer engagement and drive conversions through personalized communication and robust audience segmentation. By integrating with Joy Loyalty, businesses can synchronize valuable customer data, creating highly targeted and effective marketing campaigns that resonate with customers and boost loyalty.

### Understanding the rules

Drip is an email marketing automation platform for e-commerce, specializing in personalized communication and audience segmentation. Its core capabilities include:

- **Automated Email Campaigns:** Saves time with personalized, behavior-triggered email workflows that drive conversions.
- **Data-Driven Insights:** Tracks customer behavior to create targeted campaigns, improving relevance and engagement.
- **Segmentation and Personalization:** Builds tailored messaging for specific customer groups, leading to higher ROI.
- **E-commerce Integrations:** Syncs seamlessly with Shopify, WooCommerce, and more to use real-time store data effectively.
- **Onsite Campaigns:** Converts website visitors into subscribers and customers with forms, quizzes, and gamification.

### What Data Does Joy Loyalty Sync to Drip?

The following customer data is synced from Joy Loyalty to Drip, enabling you to create more personalized and effective campaigns:

- **Point Balance:** Show customers their current points to encourage redemptions.
- **Member Status:** Identify whether a customer is enrolled in the loyalty program.
- **Referral Link:** Include referral links in emails to boost word-of-mouth marketing.
- **Vip Tier:** Highlight VIP status and offer exclusive rewards to premium members.
- **Birthday:** Send personalized birthday greetings and special offers.
