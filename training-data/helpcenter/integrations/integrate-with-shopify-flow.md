Q: Why is Integrate With Shopify Flow not working?
A: This feature is available for **Professional, Advanced, and Enterprise plans**

The integration between Joy and Shopify Flow allows merchants to build custom automations to reward customers with points under certain conditions.

If you do not have Shopify flow app, install it [here](https://apps.shopify.com/flow).

### How to integrate with Shopify flow?

1

Access Integrations menu

- At this step, you need to access **Joy Loyalty** > **Integrations** > select **Shopify Flow**

2

### FAQs

#### I added the trigger or action from Joy to Shopify Flow, but it is running with errors and not working?

Make sure you enabled the integration toggle in our app just like the Step 2 above. This is a common problem with merchants integrating with Shopify Flow. Go to Integrations > Shopify Flow and make sure that it is enabled.
