Q: What is Visit Website Popup in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Visit Website Popup?
Q: How do I customize Visit Website Popup on my store?
A: This feature is available for **All plans**

### What is "Visit website popup" feature?

The Visit Website Popup is your digital signpost – a strategic tool designed to guide customers through your loyalty program and highlight key information the moment they land on your site. It's like a friendly navigator that ensures customers never miss out on valuable opportunities.

### Why do you need Visit website popup?

A visit website popup boosts engagement by guiding customers to key pages and increasing loyalty program visibility. It enhances your brand, drives traffic, and encourages interaction with clear, strategic messaging.

### How to set up the visit website popup

1

In the **Joy Loyalty Program** app, go to **Branding**

2

Select **Visit Website Popup** and click **Setup**

3

**Customize the popup in two following areas:**

- **Popup Content Settings:** Define content for both **Before Sign-In** and **After Sign-In** states, ensuring clarity and engagement.
- **Popup Appearance Settings:**

  - Choose the button icon that represents your brand’s style.
  - Position the popup for maximum visibility (e.g., bottom-right corner).

4

Preview and save the changes to enable the popup notification.
