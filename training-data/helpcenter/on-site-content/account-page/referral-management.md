Q: What is Referral Management in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Referral Management?
Q: How do I customize Referral Management on my store?
A: This feature is available in **Advanced, Enterprise plans**

### What is **Referral Management?**

A tool that enables customers to generate and share unique referral links with friends, monitor which contacts have signed up, track referral rewards earned, and see the status of pending referralsâ€”all from their account dashboard.

### Why you need this feature?

Referral Management transforms customers into advocates by letting them easily share personalized links with friends. The feature tracks referral statuses, building customer excitement as they watch their rewards accumulate.

### How to set up **Referral Management?**

1

**Access Embedded Content**

- Open the **Joy Loyalty** app.
- Navigate to **Embedded Content** from the dashboard.

2

**Click Account page, select Referral Management**

- Click **Edit in Theme Editor**

3

**Add the Block to Your Loyalty Page**

- Open the **Theme Editor**
- Select **Add Block**, then find **Joy: Referral Management** under the **Apps** section

4

Edit the content and color

5

Click save
