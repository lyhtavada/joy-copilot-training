Q: What is Rewards Redemption in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Rewards Redemption?
Q: How do I customize Rewards Redemption on my store?
A: This feature is available in **Advanced, Enterprise plans**

### What is Rewards Redemption?

A feature that displays available rewards, point costs, and eligibility requirements directly on the customer's account page, allowing them to browse and redeem rewards instantly without navigating elsewhere.

### Why do you need this feature?

It increases redemption rates and reducing friction in the rewards process. When redemption is simple and visible, customers are more likely to use their points, creating a positive feedback loop that encourages further engagement and purchases. Easy redemption also demonstrates the tangible value of your loyalty program, reinforcing customer participation.
