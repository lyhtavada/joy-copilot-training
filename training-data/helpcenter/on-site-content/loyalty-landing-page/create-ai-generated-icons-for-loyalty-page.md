Q: What is Create AI Generated Icons For Loyalty Page in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Create AI Generated Icons For Loyalty Page?
Q: How do I customize Create AI Generated Icons For Loyalty Page on my store?
A: This feature is available for all plans.

### What Are AI-Generated Icons for loyalty page?

AI-generated icons are custom visual elements created by artificial intelligence specifically for your loyalty program. The system uses your descriptions and preferences to generate unique icons that represent different earning activities like purchases, referrals, or social media engagement.

### Why do you need this feature?

AI-generated icons offer a powerful way to enhance your loyalty program's visual appeal without needing design expertise

- **Brand Consistency**: Create icons that perfectly match your store's visual identity
- **Time Efficiency**: Generate professional-looking icons in seconds without design skills
- **Customization**: Get unique visuals tailored specifically to your loyalty activities
- **Flexibility**: Easily regenerate or modify icons until they match your vision
- **Uniqueness**: Stand out from competitors using default loyalty program icons

### How to create AI-generated icons?

1

Navigate to 'Embedded Content' then click "Set up" in the program you want to customize

You can choose one of these programs:

- Ways to earn
- Ways to redeem
- VIP tier benefits

2

Choose "Customize icon with AI" from the options

3

Configure your icon specifications

- **Search icon**: Select a relevant category (e.g., points, rewards, referral)
- **Icon prompt**: Describe your desired icon (e.g., "gift box with sparkles")
- **Choose icon style**: Select a style that matches your brand aesthetic
- **Icon colors**: Set colors that complement your store's color scheme

4

Click "**Generate icon**" to create your custom design

5

Review the result and click "**Save**" to apply it or generate alternatives until satisfied

### FAQs:

1. **Will my icon look different on mobile devices?**

The icons are designed to be responsive and will maintain their appearance across devices, though they may be displayed at different sizes.

1. **How many different icon styles are available?**

Joy offers 5 distinct icon styles: Colored Fill Outline, Colored Outline, Doodle Art Fill, Doodle Art Fill Offset, Broken Line Art. Each style creates a different visual effect to best complement your brand aesthetic.

1. **Can I revert to the default icons if I don't like my custom ones?**

Yes, you can always return to the default icons by selecting that option in the icon customization menu.
