Q: What is Ways To Earn in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Ways To Earn?
Q: How do I customize Ways To Earn on my store?
A: This feature is available in **all plans**.

### What is "Ways to earn" feature?

The Ways to Earn feature is a transparent communication tool that showcases all available methods for customers to accumulate points or rewards in your loyalty program. This block presents earning opportunities in an organized, easy-to-understand format, helping customers maximize their program participation through various activities like purchases, referrals, and social media engagement.

### Why do you need this feature?

The "Ways to Earn" block is crucial for a loyalty page because it clearly explains how customers can accumulate points or rewards, motivating them to engage with the program. By outlining the various earning opportunities, such as purchases, referrals, or social media actions, it encourages customers to take action and participate more actively. This transparency increases program participation and helps drive customer loyalty.
