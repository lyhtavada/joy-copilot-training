Q: What is Sign Up Banner in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Sign Up Banner?
Q: How do I customize Sign Up Banner on my store?
A: - Basic features are available for: **Free** plans.
- Enhanced features are available for: **Professional, Advanced, Enterprise** plans.

### What is sign-up banner?

A sign-up banner is a prominent element displayed on your website that encourages visitors to join your loyalty program. It typically appears at the top, bottom, or as a slide-in on your pages, making it easy for customers to register without navigating away from their shopping experience.

### Why do you need sign-up banner?

Sign-up banners capture potential loyalty members at the moment of engagement. They transform casual browsers into program participants without disrupting their shopping experience, increasing visibility and enrollment rates.

By embedding these prompts within your pages, you collect valuable customer data earlier while providing immediate value through welcome offers, ultimately driving higher program participation and better return on your loyalty investment.

### How to set up sign-up banner?

1

Navigate to **Embedded Content** and go to **Loyalty page**.

2

In **sign-up banner** section, click on **Edit in Theme Editor.**

3

Choose **Add section** in **Template** section. Then, look for **Joy: Sign-up banner** in the list of blocks
