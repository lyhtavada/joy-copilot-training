Q: What is Visit Website in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How do I set up Visit Website?
Q: How do customers earn rewards with Visit Website?
A: This feature is available for **Free, Professional, Advanced, and Enterprise** plans

### What is "Visit website" feature?

The Visit Website feature is an automated engagement tool that rewards customers for actively visiting and engaging with your store's website. This innovative feature automatically tracks customer visits and distributes points, creating a seamless reward experience that encourages regular site engagement.

### Why do you need this feature?

Visit Website earning rule is an innovative addition to the Joy Loyalty program, designed to reward customers for engaging with your store. By incentivizing website visits, you can drive more traffic, enhance customer satisfaction, and strengthen loyalty in a seamless and automated way. This feature ensures that every customer interaction counts toward building a lasting relationship with your brand.

### How to set up this feature

1

**Get access to "Visit website"**

Choose **Reward programs** -> **Earning programs** -> Click **Add rule** -> Choose **Visit website**

2

**Set up "Visit website"**

- **Program name**: Enter a name that resonates with your audience (e.g., "Earn Points for Visiting")
- **Start date** and **End date**: Define the activity duration for your program
- **Earning points**: Specify the number of points customers will earn for each visit
- **Earning points limit**: Control the frequency of rewards by setting limits (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly)

3

Press **Turn on** to activate the feature
