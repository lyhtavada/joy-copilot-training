Q: What is Migration in Joy Loyalty?
Q: How does Migration work?
A: **Who can use Migration?**

- This feature is available for: **Professional,** **Advanced and Enterprise** plans.

### **Introduction**

Migrating your customer data to Joy Loyalty enables a seamless transition from your existing loyalty program, preserving all essential information. We support data migration from a wide range of platforms, ensuring a smooth and efficient process. Whether you're moving from an app like Smile, Rivo, Yotpo, or others, our step-by-step guides will walk you through the entire process.

### Which Customer Data Can Joy Migrate?

When migrating to Joy Loyalty, we support the following customer data:

- **Customer Name**: All customer names will be transferred exactly as they appear in your current system, ensuring personalization remains intact.
- **VIP Tier**: Any customer tier data, including VIP statuses, will be migrated over, preserving the current status of your loyalty members and their progress.
- **Birth Date**: Keep all customers' birth dates for special rewards and birthday campaigns. This data is crucial for maintaining personalized customer interactions.
- **Email**: Customer emails will be migrated to maintain the core communication channel between you and your customers. This ensures continued engagement and smooth identification in your loyalty program.
- **Customer Status:** Each customer's current status will be transferred accurately, whether they are a member, a guest, or have left the program. This ensures proper tracking and classification of your customers within the loyalty program.
- **Point Balance:** All point balances will be migrated seamlessly, preserving each customer’s accumulated points for future redemptions and rewards. No progress will be lost during the transfer.
- **Phone:** Customer phone numbers will be migrated to maintain essential contact details.

By migrating this data, you ensure your customers’ loyalty profiles remain accurate and up-to-date, allowing you to continue offering rewards without disruption.
