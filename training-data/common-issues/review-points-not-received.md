A: There are several requirements for the review points program to work correctly. Let's go through them:

1. **Confirm the review app is connected.** Go to Joy app > Reward programs and check that a "Write reviews" program exists and is enabled. The review app (e.g., Judge.me, Loox, Ali Reviews, etc.) must be properly connected to Joy.

2. **Both apps must be on paid plans.** This is a critical requirement! Both the Joy app **and** the review app must be on **paid plans** for the integration to work. If either app is on a free plan, points will not be awarded for reviews.

3. **The review must be published.** Joy only awards points after a review is **published** (approved), not when it's submitted or still in a pending/moderation state. Ask the merchant to check if the review has been approved and published in their review app.

4. **If all of the above are correct** but points are still not being awarded, this needs further investigation. Please provide:
   - The name of the review app being used
   - The plan the review app is on
   - The plan Joy is on

5. **Workaround:** While waiting for a resolution, the merchant can **manually adjust the customer's points** in Joy to compensate for the missing review reward.
